About Spotifyndr

Spotifyndr is a music discovery platform that connects people through their shared love of music. Using machine learning algorithms, we help you find your "music tribe" - people who have similar taste in artists, genres, and tracks.

Our Mission

We believe that music is better when shared. Our mission is to create a community where music enthusiasts can discover new music, connect with like-minded individuals, and share their favorite tracks in real-time.

How It Works

  • Connect with Spotify: Sign in with your Spotify account to import your music profile
  • ML-Powered Matching: Our algorithms analyze your listening history to find similar users
  • Discover & Connect: Browse recommendations, swipe on profiles, and message your matches
  • Share Music: Send tracks, playlists, and recommendations to friends in real-time

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is important to us. We use Spotify's OAuth authentication, which means we never see your password. Your music data is used solely for finding matches and improving recommendations. You can revoke access at any time through your Spotify account settings.
