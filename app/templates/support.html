Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Spotify account?

Click the "Sign in with Spotify" button on the landing page. You'll be redirected to Spotify's authorization page where you can grant Spotifyndr access to your music profile.

What data does Spotifyndr access?

We access your top artists, top tracks, and recently played music to find users with similar taste. We never access your password or payment information.

How do I revoke Spotifyndr's access?

You can revoke access at any time through your Spotify account settings under "Apps". This won't delete your Spotifyndr account but will stop data syncing.

How are recommendations generated?

We use machine learning algorithms (KNN similarity and logistic regression) to analyze your music profile and find users with similar taste. The more you use the app, the better recommendations become.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account from the Settings page. This will permanently delete your data from our servers.
